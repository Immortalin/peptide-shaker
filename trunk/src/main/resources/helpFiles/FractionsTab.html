Introduction

The Fractions tab shows you can overview of which fractions the proteins and peptides were detected in.

The values above are estimations of the confidence of a peptide/protein if found in a fraction alone in the context of the whole analysis.

These are not equal the confidence in the peptide/protein identifications when processing the fractions independently!

Independant fractions (like different donors, measurements) or replicates should be processed separately.

To ensure comparable fractions, verify that the PSM PEP against score plots are similar for all the fractions in the Validation tab.
