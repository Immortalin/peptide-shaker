PeptideShaker

visualization of proteomics identifications for multiple search engines

PeptideShaker is divided into tabs covering different aspects of the data analysis:
  • Overview: get a simple but detailed overview of the protein and peptide identifications.
  • Spectrum IDs: compare search engine performance and see how the search engine results are combined.
  • Modifications: get an overview of the post-translational modifications in the dataset.
  • 3D Structure: map the detected peptides onto the corresponding PDB structures.
  • Annotation get simple access to protein annotation resources for your protein.
  • Validation: inspect the calculations behind the identifications.
  • QC Plots inspect the quality of the indentifications.

A PeptideShaker tutorial explaining the most common features is available at the PeptideShaker home page.

At the PeptideShaker home page you will also find a Troubleshooting section dealing with issues related to the startup and the running of the tool.

Need additional help? Look for the question mark icons.
