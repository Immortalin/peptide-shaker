Population

The standard populations inspected by PeptideShaker are PSMs, peptides and proteins. However, if statistical significance is ensured PSMs will be separated according to their charge. Similarly, peptides can be separated based on their modification status.




Optimize Estimator Accuracy

The estimators plot will help you to optimize the estimators. Fluctuations in the PEP illustrate a lack of robustness whereas disagreement of FDR estimators shows a lack of sensitivity. This can be corrected by optimizing the PEP window (directly or using the slide bars). By default, the PEP window is set to a minimal value Nmax: the maximum amount of target hits comprised between two subsequent decoy hits.

Sometimes the PEP cannot be accurately estimated, for small populations for instance. The confidence and probabilistic estimators will then not be reliable anymore. It is adviced to trust only score and classical FDR.




Setting the Threshold

The thresholds plots allow you to optimize the thresold. Depending on the experimentalist's aims, a balance can be found between quality and quantity. The thresold can be set manually using a minimal confidence threshold, a maximal allowed FDR or a minimal allowed FNR.
